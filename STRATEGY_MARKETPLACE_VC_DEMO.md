### Q: "How do you handle strategy performance degradation?"
**A:** "Excellent question. Strategy performance can degrade due to market regime changes. Here's our approach:

1. **Real-Time Monitoring**: Our composite scoring algorithm recalculates every 30 seconds. If a strategy's Sharpe Ratio drops or Max Drawdown increases, it automatically drops in the rankings.

2. **Transparent Metrics**: We show customers the recent performance (7d, 30d, 90d, YTD returns). They can see if a strategy is degrading.

3. **Automatic Repricing**: As strategies drop in rankings, we can move them to lower tiers. For example, if Deep Learning drops below 75 composite score, we might move it from Professional ($249) to Standard ($149) tier.
