Platform Features

Portfolio Dashboard (Bottom Right)

Look at the bottom-right corner to see the real-time portfolio dashboard. It shows live updates of your portfolio value, individual holdings, and performance metrics. The values update every 2 seconds to simulate real market conditions.

Model Transparency (Bottom Left)

Check the bottom-left corner for the model transparency panel. It displays the AI model's current accuracy, trading signals with confidence levels, and the top features being used for predictions.
