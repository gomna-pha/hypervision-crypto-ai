🧠 Algorithm Deep Dive

🌐 Hyperbolic Geometry Foundation
Our system uses the Poincaré disk model to embed financial assets in hyperbolic space. Unlike Euclidean space, hyperbolic geometry allows for exponential growth of space, making it ideal for modeling complex financial relationships where small price movements can lead to large arbitrage opportunities.
⚡ Arbitrage Detection Process
1. Asset Embedding: Each asset is positioned in hyperbolic space based on price, volume, and volatility.
2. Geodesic Calculation: We compute shortest paths between assets using hyperbolic metrics.
3. Opportunity Scoring: Paths with high curvature indicate potential arbitrage opportunities.
4. Risk Assessment: Distance from the disk center represents risk level.
🔬 Mathematical Framework
Poincaré Metric: ds² = 4(dx² + dy²)/(1 - x² - y²)²
Möbius Transformations: Used for rotation and scaling operations
Geodesic Distance: d(z₁, z₂) = arcosh(1 + 2|z₁ - z₂|²/((1 - |z₁|²)(1 - |z₂|²)))
